Welcome to Hamachi the Psychotic Killer! The platformer game where you can play as the BAD GUY.

Hamachi the Psychotic Killer is a platform game where you take on the role of Hamachi, a civilian who just happened to be in the wrong place at the wrong time, now under control by a demonic force who wishes to influence him to take innocent lives in order to conquer the world. Your goal is to kill everything in your path.. If you choose to. Every character you encounter isn't just an obstacle, they're part of a living world. You can choose to spare or kill them, shaping your path into one of the three routes: Pacifist, Neutral, or Genocide. You can also spare the main bosses, who will then join your party to help Hamachi defeat the main villain: Demon Queen Deity

If you decide to abide by the Demons wishes, then you will slaughter innocent civilians and paint the town red with Hamachi’s weapon of choice: the axe. Occasionally, you’ll come across a chainsaw that grants invincibility and kills anything you touch!

With blood fresh on your hands you're now wanted by the police. Run away, or they will catch you.

This game is in development and the current iteration of early access offers 4 playable worlds, and two Challenge Levels.
