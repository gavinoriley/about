In this platformer, the roles are reversed. Instead of playing the good guy, you’re the bad guy, and your goal is to kill everything in your path… but of course, you don’t have to.

Slaughter innocent civilians and paint the town red with Hamachi’s weapon of choice: the axe. Occasionally, you’ll come across a chainsaw that grants invincibility and instantly kills anything in your path!

But with blood fresh on your hands you're now wanted by the police. You better run, or they will catch you!

This game is still in the early access phase with 3 worlds available for play! You can download the demo or buy the early access version to support me while I continue to develop it.
