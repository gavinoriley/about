In this platformer, instead of playing the good guy, you're the bad guy, and the goal is to kill everything in your path.. but of course you don’t have to..

Slaughter innocent civilians and paint the town red with Hamachi’s weapon of choice: the axe. Occasionally, you’ll come across a chainsaw that grants invincibility and kills anything you touch!

With blood fresh on your hands you're now wanted by the police. You better run, or they will catch you!

This game is in development and offers an early access build with 3 playable worlds. You can download the demo or purchase the early access version to support me while I continue development. Early access buyers will receive the full version of the game upon release.
