In this platformer, you control a piece of tofu named Hamachi, who unexpectedly falls into the possession of Demon Queen Deity, an evil fiend who intends to use him as a vessel to conquer the world through mass murder.

features:

  • Play as the villain: Choose to kill, spare, or ignore every civilian.
  • Three distinct routes: Pacifist, Neutral, and Genocide, each with its own ending.
  • Defeat or pacify demonic bosses, pacified bosses will join your side.
  • Journey through 8 visually distinct worlds built with original pixel art.
  • Chainsaw power-up grants temporary invincibility and destroys any being you touch.
  • Collect souls from dead civilians or sushi dropped by Penelope Pom Pom Pink to restore health.
  • Your crimes will not go unnoticed by the authorities.

The platformer game where you can play as the BAD GUY. (or the good guy)

Venture through a world where your actions have consequences.
In this platformer, you control a piece of tofu named Hamachi, who unexpectedly falls into the possession of Demon Queen Deity, an evil fiend who intends to use him as a vessel to conquer the world through mass murder.

Spare or kill innocent civilians with your axe, or with a chainsaw that grants invincibility!

Fight against powerful demons, or show them you mean now harm and pacify them!

Your crimes will not go unnoticed by the authorities.
